Introduction of the Pharmacy

"Raini Pharmacy" was established on 20thOctober 2016 near the Karapitiya hospital.Till December of 2019 it was a great business to seller.
After Covid19 spread throughout the country all business men had to face big problems. In pharmacy field,daily thousands of visitors come to visit the pharmacy before
Covid19. But now days people afraid to visit the pharmacy as the current situation. As these reason we are going to make on online ordering system to pharmacy.
In these system customer can order their required medicine and get deliver facilities to their door step. They will receive their invoice and they can pay through online
or after delivered medicines to their hand.
This system will help to develop the management of system. And customers will again start to buy medicines more than old days. According to the current situation, people
in Galle area will be get touch of this system and lot of customers will be circled through the system.
